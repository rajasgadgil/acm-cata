The Windria plugin let's you embed an interactive weather map onto your website, visualizing precise wind/weather/wave/rain forecasts for anywhere in the world.

== Description ==

Show your visitors wind/weather/waves/currents/rain etc. on a precise, beautiful, interactive map! Perfect for any business that is weather related (e.g. marinas, yacht club, paragliding, ...)

To embed Windria, simply use this shortcode: [windria url="brighton-gb" width="800" height="500"] ( replace “brigthon-gb” with the url for your location, which you can check by searching here: https://windria.net )

Windria is completely free for you to embed on your site and visualizes the most precise weather information available, anywhere on the planet.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `windria.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to https://windria.net and search for your location. Note the url: e.g. https://windria.net/london-gb
4. Embed your parking map using our shortcode: [windria url="london-gb" width="800" height="500"]
